ATTENDEE NOTES

Checking In

Proceed to the 11th Floor. The front desk will buzz in. You can register and pay (if not already paid) on entry

Recording Of Sessions

All sessions will be recorded and made available on a You Tube channel after the conference

Payment

You can pay via Pay Pal prior to the conference or at the door with Square.

If you have made arrangements with the organizer, he will be at the payment table and can discuss it with you.

Food and Drinks

Our sponsors will provide Breakfast, Lunch and a Snack at the end of the day

Coffee and snacks will be available through out the day in the kitch on the main floor

Phone calls and Device Charging

There will be a charge area on the 11th floor and an area on the 10th to socialize through out the day

Questions And Concerns

Please go to the front desk on the 11th with concerns or questions

Also look for the conference staff, they will be wearing white Spring t-shirts

Washrooms

Washrooms are located on the both floors of the conference
